Version 1.0

Our first offically released version. Cool virtual reality viewer features including:

  • a nice start page where you enter the virtual scenes
  • 8 campus scenes and switch between them with zoom-in effect
  • nice cube spheres guide you through the entire virtual tour
  • audio introduction compatible on both iOS and android
  • all virtual tour designed both for PC and google cardboard
  • configurable file makes it easier to add more content in the future
  • lots of back-end speed optimization

Version 1.1

  • add "back to start" function when lookng down on the ground
